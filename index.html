Week 1 : Short Bio

An area in my life that I feel represents me the most is likely going to be the gym. Every since I was 13 years old, I have always had the
motivation of becoming the best version of myself. Going to the gym consistently for over a decade now has really tought me how to be disciplined in what I do.
For example; if I have a goal in mind, I will do all I can to achieve that goal. I will stay disciplined until that goal is achieved or close to it. With that being said, being self disciplined
allows one to subconsciously improve their overall well being in life, either through happiness or motivation to get something done.
