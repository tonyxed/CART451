Week 1 : Short Bio

An area in my life that I feel represents me the most is likely going to be the gym. Every since I was 13 years old, I have always had the
motivation of becoming the best version of myself. Going to the gym consistently for over a decade now has really tought me how to be disciplined in what I do.
For example; if I have a goal in mind, (either be losing weight or gaining weight) I will do all I can to achieve that goal. I will stay disciplined until that goal is achieved or close to it.
With that being said, being self disciplined allows one to subconsciously improve their overall well being in life, either through means of happiness or motivation to get something done.
Going to the gym can also help those to become less anxious, more confident and boost one's self-esteem which in return helps these individuals to use these practices in their everyday lives to get ahead of their peers.
Consequently, these practices not only propelled my confidence but my ability to achieve whatever I want to achieve in life, which in return has helped me to become who I am today.
